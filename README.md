# bards-song
A music player for your study adventure.

🔥 Bard's Song
A cozy, pixel-art inspired music player built with Electron. Sit by the campfire and let the music play.

Inspiration

Bard's Song was inspired by a music player project by YouTube creator @itsbeuina. I used their video as a foundation and am building on top of it with my own ideas, aesthetic choices, and features.
Current Features

Drag and drop local audio files (MP3, WAV, FLAC, OGG) to play
Play, pause, previous, and next controls
Progress bar
Cozy campfire pixel art background
Frameless window with minimize and close buttons

Planned Features

🎵 Music Service Integration

Connect to Apple Music and/or Spotify to stream directly from your library
Apple Music integration is the primary target

✨ Visual Effects

Particle effects simulating sparks flickering from the campfire in the background image

🎨 UI Redesign

Replace placeholder text buttons with pixel art button images

Rework the overall layout — considering a split design with the background image on top and a dedicated control panel section on the bottom

Decide whether to display album/cover art for the currently playing track

General UI polish and improvements
